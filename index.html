Who We Are

Established in 2016 under corporate laws of Niger, Innov Construction Afrik SARL has grown into a highly trusted partner for complex civil engineering, structural restoration, and specialized urban architecture.

Holding an official 3rd Category government construction certification, we safely engineer assets that sustain commercial development, support critical state infrastructure, and withstand the dynamic climatic demands of the Sahel region.
